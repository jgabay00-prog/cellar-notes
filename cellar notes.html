Your tasting journal syncs across all your devices via Google Drive. Sign in once to get started.
🔑
One-time setup
You need a free Google OAuth client ID. This takes about 3 minutes and is only done once.
1
Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2
Create a new project — name it Cellar Notes (or anything you like).
3
Go to APIs & Services → Library. Search for and enable the Google Drive API.
4
Go to APIs & Services → OAuth consent screen. Choose External, fill in app name (Cellar Notes), your email, and save. Add your Google email as a test user.
5
Go to Credentials → Create Credentials → OAuth client ID. Choose Web application. Under Authorized JavaScript origins add the exact URL you open this file from (e.g. file:// or your hosted URL). Copy the Client ID.
6
Paste it below and tap Connect.
