One-time setup
You need a free Google OAuth Client ID. This takes about 3 minutes and only happens once.
1
Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2
Create a new project — name it Cellar Notes.
3
Go to APIs & Services → Library, search for and enable the Google Drive API.
4
Go to APIs & Services → OAuth consent screen. Choose External, fill in the app name and your email. Add your email as a test user.
5
Go to Credentials → Create Credentials → OAuth client ID. Choose Web application. Under Authorized JavaScript origins add https://jgabay00-prog.github.io. Copy the Client ID.
6
Paste it below and tap Connect.
